My Services

Single Coaching

I offer professional and personalized coaching lessons to help you improve your overall ingame performance. Before the coaching lessons start, we have a talk or a chat with each other, so I can customize the lesson specifically for you, so you get the most out of it. I will help you build on your strengths and work on your skill, so you can convert my lessons in the most effective way into your own gameplay.

Team Coaching:

Since I was part of a professional team and have scrimmed alot of times against the best teams of the world (Dignitas, Fnatic), I also offer teamcoaching. A team coaching can consist for example one of the following points: Team Coordination and communication, and how to build a team spirit.




To give you an overall example, almost all coaching lessons consist the following schema:

Consulting:

 What is your overall goal, in which aspects do you want to improve?

Analysing:

Watching a replay to see where your mistakes are.

Coaching:

 Actively working on your mistakes and your gameplay, for example in a custom game together

Conclusion:

 Identifying what your next step will be, for example practicing a certain hero for X hours a day
